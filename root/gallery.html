Project 5: Concept Art

Project Description

This is concept art for Susurrus, a game I have been planning for a few years. Susurrus is about a small child looking for her grandma, who she believes was taken by a UFO. Since the game is from the perspective of a child, I tried to reflect that in the art style and used thick lines and a brush that looks like a marker.
