Project 4: Asteroid Game

Project Description

Using the Phaser game engine and Javascript, I programmed a prototype game about saving a planet from asteroids by destroying them with a UFO.

The player controls the UFO's movements, and has to keep track of the "shields" that lose percentage after hitting asteroids and regenerates over time. The UFO also keeps moving unless it hits an asteroid, to replicate the lack of gravity in space.

For the asteroids, I used a finite-state machine to make their direction and velocity random each time they appear. More asteroids will appear if they are all destroyed, and they also appear on a timer.

The player wins if they have a score of at least 500, a shield percentage of at least 5%, and all asteroids on the screen are destroyed.
